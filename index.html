About

Lindsay

I’m an undergraduate with 9+ years of experience in software, hardware, and network support, as well as application and web development. I have worked for major companies such as Alberta Health Services, Calgary Airport Authority, IBM, DeVry and Comcast Cable. I have also volunteered with the student group Enactus DeVry, aiding the team in a development project as a junior programmer.


Colleagues know me as a highly motivated individual that will see projects through until the end. I know that client’s business comes first, and I apply my skills to not only aid the client with their needs but also help the team as a whole. I can work well alone, but I’m at my best collaborating with others.


I have a B.Sc in Computer Information Systems from DeVry Institute of Technology.
